## Q: V10 MessageSource与V9 Feature对应关系

**问题描述**：咨询V10版本的V2NIMMessageSource与V9版本的NIMMessageFeature如何对应。

**答案**：V2NIM_MESSAGE_SOURCE_OFFLINE对应kNIMMessageFeatureLeaveMsg，V2NIM_MESSAGE_SOURCE_ROAMING对应kNIMMessageFeatureRoamMsg，V2NIM_MESSAGE_SOURCE_ONLINE包含kNIMMessageFeatureSyncMsg+kNIMMessageFeatureDefault
